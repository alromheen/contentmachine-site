Silent Battles Publisher

A simple publishing tool for creators who generate short motivational videos and choose which videos to upload or publish to their TikTok account.

What The App Does

Silent Battles Publisher is part of the ContentMachine project. It helps users prepare short-form videos, captions, hashtags, and publishing metadata. When a user authorizes TikTok, the app can upload or publish only the videos selected by that user.

How TikTok Authorization Is Used

TikTok authorization is used to connect a user's TikTok account to the app. The app uses this access only to support user-requested video upload or publishing actions.

  • Users choose which video should be uploaded or published.
  • The app does not post without user authorization.
  • Users can revoke access from their TikTok account settings.
  • Users can request deletion of their app-related data.
