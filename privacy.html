Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Silent Battles Publisher is a tool that helps users create short motivational videos and upload or publish selected videos to their TikTok account after authorization.

Information We Collect

When a user connects TikTok or uses the app, the app may collect or process:

  • TikTok basic profile information provided through TikTok authorization.
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens needed to connect to TikTok.
  • Uploaded video metadata, including selected video file name, title, captions, hashtags, platform, and status.
  • App usage logs, such as upload attempts, timestamps, success status, and error messages.

How We Use Information

The app uses this information to:

  • Authenticate the user's TikTok account.
  • Upload or publish videos selected by the user.
  • Create captions, hashtags, and publishing records.
  • Diagnose technical errors and maintain publication logs.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not publish videos unless the user authorizes the action.
  • We do not use TikTok access for unrelated activity.

Data Sharing

Videos and publishing metadata are sent to TikTok only when needed to upload or publish selected content. We may use service providers needed to operate the app infrastructure, but we do not sell user data.

OAuth Tokens

OAuth tokens are used to connect the user's TikTok account to the app. Users can revoke access at any time from their TikTok account settings. After access is revoked, the app may no longer be able to upload or publish to that TikTok account.

Data Retention And Deletion

App-related metadata and logs may be retained as needed to operate the app, troubleshoot errors, and keep publication records. Users can request deletion of their app-related data by contacting us.

How To Revoke TikTok Access

Users can revoke app access from their TikTok account settings. The exact menu may vary by TikTok version, but it is typically found under security, privacy, or connected apps settings.
